The Range Rover Velar is known for its sleek and contemporary exterior design, blending luxury with a hint of ruggedness typical of Land Rover vehicles.

The front grille of the Velar is often characterized by the distinctive Range Rover design, featuring a mesh pattern or slats depending on the trim level.
The grille is flanked by sleek LED headlights, giving the vehicle a bold and commanding presence on the road.

The body of the Velar features dynamic contours and sculpted surfaces that catch the light in different ways, accentuating its premium design and giving it a sense of movement even when stationary.







The Velar has TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine combines two turbochargers with Valvetronic fully variable valve control and the direct petrol injection High-Precision Injection. It achieves a peak output of 390 kW (530 hp) with a maximum torque of 750 Nm. Both turbochargers are positioned neatly between the cylinder banks.
